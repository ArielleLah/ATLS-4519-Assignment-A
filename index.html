Marine Corps Boot Camp Preview

Marine Corps Boot camp is either in MCRD Parris Island or Marine Corps Recruit Depot San Diego. You train for 13 weeks, learning a variety of tests that include Marine Corps Martial Arts, Swim Qualification, Rifle Range, field combat skills and Marine Corps history. At the end of training every recruit finnishes The Crucible and earns their Eagle, Globe and Anchor. Insider Video of Marine Bootcamp
